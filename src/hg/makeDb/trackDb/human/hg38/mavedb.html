Description

This supertrack provides heatmaps of multiplexed assays of variant effects (MAVE) from MaveDB. Each heatmap presents the results of an experiment where many small substitutions were tested within a gene to examine their functional consequences. Accompanying tracks display alignments of each experiment sequence to the genome.

Data Access

Direct access to the data files for these experiments can be obtained from MaveDB.
