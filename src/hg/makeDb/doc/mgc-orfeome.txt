MGC was a project generate full-length, unrestricted mRNA clones for several
species.  ORFeome was a related project that carried on the work.  The tracks
for these two projects are generated automatically by the GenBank pipeline
based on keywords.

This is a summary of assemblies contain the MGC and ORFeome tracks and was
created just to help combine the tracks into a super track for certain
assemblies.  Combination with trackDb override didn't work, so
../../trackDb.mgc-orfeome.ra is used when both are present.
