advanced search tips
  1. + (plus) sign before +word to force included in result
  2. - (minus) sign before -word to exclude word from result
  3. * (asterisk) character at end of word* to make the word be a prefix search
  4. enclose "words in quotes" to form a phrase for the search
  5. all searches are case insensitive
  6. single word searches become prefix searches if the word does not match by itself
